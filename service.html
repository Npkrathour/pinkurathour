Our process

Here's a quick look at how we work. We know that intricate processes can be discouraging, so we've designed ours to be straightforward and hassle-free.

1

Scoping session

Starting with an easy-going discussion is ideal. We'll get to know your business, understand your timeline, your needs and plan when you'd like your new website to go live.

2

Proposal

You've shared your goals - now let us outline the plan. We'll give you a clear breakdown of costs and timelines. We'll also cover deadlines and any other key details to ensure we're all on the same page.

3

Strategy

Our strategy phase identifies your target customers and their needs. We then plan the steps to build a website that attracts them, ensuring it includes only the essentials.

4

Build

We'll begin with the design phase, using the content you've provided. Once you approve the design and everyone is satisfied, we'll move on to building it in Webflow.

5

Deliver

The website is ready to launch! Instead of simply sending over the finished project, we'd love to show it to you first. Let's schedule a call to unveil your website together.
